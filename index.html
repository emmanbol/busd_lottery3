How to Play Lottery

- Input two numbers between 0 and 9 (inclusive). You can play multiple entry.

- Lottery runs daily starting at 11AM GMT and ends by 10:30am next day.

- Once Draw ends, The 2 lucky Number for the Draw is revealed and entries are Autoprocessed.

- Winner(s) share 70% of the Lottery Contract Balance(BUSD).

- If no Winner for a day's draw, BUSD Balance rolls to next day draw which gives room for bigger winnings.
